About

Practical technical support for growing businesses

Business Studio helps growing businesses access practical technical support without needing to hire a full-time data scientist, machine learning engineer, software engineer, data analyst, or automation specialist.

Business-first, not tool-first

We focus on the real business process first. Then we recommend the simplest useful solution, whether that is a data science report, churn analysis, predictive model, dashboard, custom software tool, or automation.

The goal is to turn data and workflows into useful decisions, reports, models, and tools.

What we value

  • Practical solutions
  • Useful data science and reporting
  • Machine learning models where they make sense
  • Clear communication
  • Business-first thinking
  • Support for non-technical teams

Who we serve

Practical support for many types of growing organizations

We work with small businesses, ecommerce teams, agencies, service businesses, clinics, logistics teams, schools, and operations teams that need better reporting, smarter predictions, useful models, tools, or automation.

Data handling

  • Your data is handled confidentially.
  • We only use the data needed for the project.
  • We can work with anonymized data where possible.
