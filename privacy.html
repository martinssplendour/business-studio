Privacy Policy

This simple policy explains what information Business Studio may collect when you contact us.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, service request details, and any information you choose to include in a message.

How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to your request, understand your business needs, prepare recommendations, and provide services you ask for.

How we protect information

We aim to keep submitted information secure and only use it for legitimate business communication and service delivery. Your data is handled confidentially, we only use the data needed for the project, and we can work with anonymized data where possible.

Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when needed to provide a requested service, comply with law, or protect our business.
